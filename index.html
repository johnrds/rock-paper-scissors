Rock, Paper Scissors Game

How to Play

  1. Click on the icon you want to play the round
  2. A random choice from the computer will appear on the right side
  3. The rule to decide the winner is pretty simple:
    • Same icon it's a draw
    • Rock crushes Scissors
    • Scissors cuts paper
    • Paper covers Rock
  4. You can see the rounds results at Results panel
